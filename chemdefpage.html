What is Chemistry?

  • Chemistry is a study that observes the properties, compositions, and structures of the building blocks of everything around us, which are called atoms. The air you breate, the food you eat, and the water you drink are all made of atoms.
  • It also deals with the behavior of substances (which are also composed of atoms) when they come into contact with each other or a certain factor that can change their properties, like heat.
  • Chemistry can even be seen in everyday life. A good example is when you cook food, because of how the composition of the food changes due to the heat of the stove.

Chemistry can be divided into 5 major branches, namely:

  • Organic Chemistry
  • Inorganic Chemistry
  • Physical Chemistry
  • Analytical Chemistry
  • Biochemistry
