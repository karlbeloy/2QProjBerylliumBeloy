Organic Chemistry

  • Organic Chemistry focuses on studying organic compounds. Organic compounds were formerly limited to compounds that are made naturally and contain Carbon, but now the category has been broadened to include man-made objects that still contain Carbon. A compound that does not contain Carbon counts as inorganic.
