Analytical Chemistry

  • Analytical Chemistry is a branch of chemistry that focuses on collecting, processing, and sending out quantitative data on the composition and structure of matter. It determines what matter is and how much of it is there.
  • An example of Ananytical Chemistry being used is determining the concentration if impurities in the water to see if it is potable and safe enough to be consumed.
